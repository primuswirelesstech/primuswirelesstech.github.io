Our process...

  • We write the script.
  • We provide walkthroughs of our storyboards.
  • We provide Style Frames to make sure we are capturing the correct tone.
  • We provide professional voice over and sound design.
  • We adhere to your brand guidelines no matter how stringent.
  • We deliver fully animated videos with all the bells and whistles in any format you need.
  • And we do it all in about 3-4 weeks, maybe sooner.

Before a project starts, we check the usage/distribution strategy. We want to see results as much as the client. That’s why we start with the end – finding out what campaign success looks like.

We’re always conscious of how far budgets can go, so if we think it’s right to sacrifice production budget for the sake of ad spend, we will tell you.
