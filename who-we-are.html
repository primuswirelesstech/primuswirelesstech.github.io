About Primus Wireless Tech

Primus Wireless Tech is a Lagos-based digital and creative production company built around one simple idea: good technology and strong storytelling should work together. We help entrepreneurs, brands, agencies and organisations turn ideas into polished digital products, campaign content and interactive experiences.

Our work brings together website design, app planning, video production, motion graphics, 2D/3D animation, game development, AR, VR and brand design. That mix allows clients to move from early idea to launch with a team that understands both the creative message and the technical delivery.

We work with clients that need practical, attractive and useful digital outcomes. Some need a professional website. Some need a product video. Others need animation, a game concept, an AR campaign, or a clearer way to present their brand. Primus helps shape the brief, plan the route and deliver work that can be used across web, mobile, social, broadcast and campaign channels.

How We Work

Every project starts with understanding the audience, the message and the result the client wants to achieve.

Clear discovery

We clarify the business goal, target audience, timeline, budget and success measure before production starts.

Creative direction

We shape scripts, storyboards, style frames, wireframes and design ideas so the final output feels aligned and professional.

Delivery focus

We build, produce, review and refine the work so it is ready for launch, campaign use or handover.

Who We Support

Primus supports startups, growing businesses, agencies, entertainment brands, e-commerce teams, churches, travel businesses and corporate teams that need creative and technical delivery support.
