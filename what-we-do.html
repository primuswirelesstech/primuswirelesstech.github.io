Website, App, Video, Animation, Game, AR and VR Services

Primus Wireless Tech is a Lagos-based digital and creative production company helping entrepreneurs, brands, agencies and businesses build a stronger digital presence. We combine software delivery, design thinking, animation, video production and interactive technology to turn ideas into useful, attractive and commercially focused outcomes.

Whether you need a new website, a mobile app concept, a product video, 2D/3D animation, a playable campaign, game development, augmented reality or virtual reality experience, we shape the idea, create the assets and support delivery from concept to launch.
